The Story

We decided to develop a story that brought awareness to abortion rights and reproductive health. In our story, there is a woman named Anne who seeks out an abortion, but unfourtunately where she lives, Texas, women can no longer get them, and she can't afford to travel across state lines for one. Out of desperation she goes to an undercover doctor, and she ends up dying.

Technology and Code

The technology and code is what I specialized in for our project. We did a motorized story box and we used makecode.adafruit.com. I originally wrote code that would utilize motors, ut after talking to Mr. Sinusas, I realized that servos would be better for making more deliberate rotations for our characters, considering they had to all be at specific places at a certain time. I had a few struggles with the code up until the day the project was due. It was a little hard to figure out which direction was which with the servos, so it involved a lot of trial and error. We soon came to find that their servos only really rotated 45 degrees. This meant we had to make a few last adjustments to the set but we worked it out, because I learned that I could set the servos at different starting points which would give the illusion that it is tuning a further distance..
